Dal Bhat TarkariDal-Bhat-Tarkari (Nepali: दाल भात तरकारी) is eaten throughout Nepal. Dal is a soup made of lentils and spices, served over boiled grain, bhat — usually rice but sometimes another grain and a vegetable curry, tarkari. Condiments are usually small amounts of spicy pickle which can be fresh or fermented, and radish.
